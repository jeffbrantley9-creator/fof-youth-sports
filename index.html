How it works

From sign-up to Saturday in three steps.

1

Pick a sport & season

Find a season near you. We post location, dates, and price up front. No hidden fees.

2

Register in five minutes

Player info, parent contact, jersey size, pay. We email you a confirmation that same day.

3

Get matched, hit the field

Coach reaches out a week before opening day with practice time. Show up. Play.
