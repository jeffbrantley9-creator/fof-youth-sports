Program Pillars

WHAT WE DEVELOP

⚡
SPEED
Linear acceleration, burst speed, and sport-specific explosive movement patterns.
🔄
AGILITY
Change of direction, reaction time, and lateral quickness that transfers to every sport.
💪
STRENGTH
Functional bodyweight strength, core stability, and joint integrity — age-appropriate always.
🎯
COORDINATION
Multi-limb coordination, rhythm, and body awareness that makes athleticism visible.
🏀
SPORT TRANSFER
Every Combine session ends by connecting training directly to live sport situations.
Founder & Program Director

COACH JEFFERY BRANTLEY

Coach Brantley brings over a decade of youth athletic administration to F.O.F. Youth Sports. As a Physical Education Teacher and Head Basketball Coach at Corvian Community School — where he led the Cardinals to the 2025 North Carolina State Basketball Championship — Coach Brantley has spent his career building athletes and communities.

His vision for F.O.F. is rooted in a simple belief: every child deserves structured, professional athletic development — not just the ones at elite academies.

SPORTS OFFERED

Something for every athlete. Every session brings a new lineup.

🏀
BASKETBALL

F.O.F.'s anchor sport. Fundamentals-first coaching covering jump stops, ball handling, scoring mindset, footwork, and team communication. All four divisions compete.

Fall A Fall B Winter A Winter B Spring
⚽
SOCCER

Dribbling, passing, positional play, and small-sided games. Outdoor sport that runs in Fall A and Spring — perfect for athletes who love movement and space.

Fall A Spring
🏈
FLAG FOOTBALL

Routes, reads, snaps, and pursuit angles. Flag football develops football IQ, coordination, and teamwork in a safe, competitive format. Fall A and Spring only.

Fall A Spring
🏐
VOLLEYBALL

Serving, passing, setting, and court positioning. Volleyball builds communication, reaction time, and spatial awareness. Runs through both Fall B and Winter sessions.

Fall B Winter A Winter B
📣
CHEER

Unified cheer program covering routines, formations, teamwork, and performance. Cheer athletes support and perform alongside other F.O.F. sports throughout the season.

Fall A Fall B Spring
⚾
TEE BALL

Hitting, fielding, base running, and love of the game. Tee Ball is designed for the youngest F.O.F. athletes — building baseball fundamentals in a supportive environment.
